The Cow: A Domestic Animal

The cow is a domestic animal that is also one of the most useful animals to humans. It provides us with many things that are essential for our survival.

Milk Production

Cows are well-known for their milk production. The milk from cows provides us with a nutritious food source that is a staple in many diets around the world.

Role in Agriculture

Cows also play a significant role in agriculture. They were used for ploughing the fields in traditional farming methods, and their dung is used as a rich source of fertilizer.

Cultural Significance

In many cultures, cows have a religious significance and are revered. They are often associated with prosperity and wealth in these cultures.
